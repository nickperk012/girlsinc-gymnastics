Group privates are for 2–3 kids together. Each child pays per-child: $20 for 30 min, $30 for 45 min, or $40 for 1 hour. Make sure all children are listed when submitting the booking request.
A leotard is ideal. Form-fitting athletic wear (leggings + tucked-in top) also works well. Bare feet or gymnastics shoes — no regular socks on the equipment floor.
Availability varies by coach and week. Use the booking calendar to see open slots. Privates are typically available Monday through Friday with limited weekend slots available by request.
